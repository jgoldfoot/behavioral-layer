Papers, where the synthesis is the value. This section covers peer-reviewed and preprint
research on agent behavior, trust, and human-agent interaction. A note here is worth
writing when it distills a paper into something a builder or an exec can act on, not when
it merely links to the abstract.

## What belongs here

- Papers on agent behavior, reasoning-and-acting, planning, and tool use.
- Research on trust, reliance, calibration, and human-agent interaction.
- Survey and methodology papers that reframe how the field thinks about behavior.

## What goes elsewhere

- Benchmarks introduced by a paper can be cross-listed in [[evaluate/index|Evaluate]].
- The behavioral concepts the research informs are defined in [[behavior/index|Behavior]].
