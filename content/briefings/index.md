The exec lens. Briefings are the weekly digest: a short, decision-oriented synthesis built
from the week's [[signal/index|Signal]] entries and any notable new resources. The reader
is someone deciding whether and how to ship agentic products, not someone implementing them.

## What belongs here

- Weekly digests that summarize what changed and why it matters for a decision-maker.
- Cross-cutting themes that span [[behavior/index|Behavior]], [[build/index|Build]],
  [[models/index|Models]], and [[evaluate/index|Evaluate]].
- The "so what" for risk, reliability, and trust, written in plain language.

## Discipline

- Each briefing links back to the primary-source notes it synthesizes.
- The exec read in every resource note is the raw material for these digests.
