Dated "what shipped / what changed" entries. Signal is the raw feed: short, time-stamped
notes on concrete events (a release, a changelog, a new benchmark result, a documented
behavior change) that matter for the behavioral layer. It is the source material the
weekly [[briefings/index|Briefings]] digest draws from.

## What belongs here

- Releases and changelog entries with a behavioral implication.
- New or updated benchmarks and notable published results.
- Documented behavior changes worth dating and tracking over time.

## Discipline

- Every signal entry is dated and cites a primary source (changelog, release post, repo).
- Keep entries short. The synthesis happens in [[briefings/index|Briefings]], not here.
