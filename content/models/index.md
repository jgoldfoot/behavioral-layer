Frontier models read through the agent-behavior lens. This section is not a model
leaderboard. It tracks what each publicly documented model means for behavior: its tool
use, its instruction-following and refusal patterns, its long-horizon reliability, and the
behavioral guarantees (or gaps) its makers document.

## What belongs here

- Public model releases and model cards, summarized for their agent-behavior implications.
- Documented changes to instruction-following, tool use, or safety behavior across versions.
- System-card findings about escalation, honesty, and failure behavior.

## What goes elsewhere

- Independent measurement of these behaviors lives in [[evaluate/index|Evaluate]].
- The frameworks that wrap these models live in [[build/index|Build]].
- Dated release notes and shipping events live in [[signal/index|Signal]].
